About the School

Panini Ashram is a government educational institution where underprivileged and needy children from across Nepal are provided with quality Sanskrit education along with residential facilities.

Main Facilities

  • Residential hostel facilities
  • Nutritious and regular meals
  • Disciplined academic environment
  • Special focus on Sanskrit education
  • Classes conducted by experienced teachers

Our Mission

Our primary goal is to preserve the Sanskrit language and culture while providing quality education to children from disadvantaged and remote areas.
