Who We Are

Panini Ashram is a government-run Sanskrit-focused educational institution that provides free education, hostel facilities, and nutritious meals to underprivileged and needy children from across Nepal. We are based in the beautiful region of Syangja-14, Lamage.

Our goal is to create a nurturing, disciplined, and spiritually enriched environment where students not only learn but grow into responsible citizens grounded in Vedic knowledge. Sanskrit, the root of ancient wisdom, is at the heart of our curriculum.
